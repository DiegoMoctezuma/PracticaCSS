Preparations Before Vaccine

The success of vaccines is strongly influenced by the strength of the body's immune system. Therefore, the are several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19 vaccine work :

Avoid alcoholic beverages

Kaplan says. “Since we do not yet know enough about the influence of alcohol on allergic reactions, I recommend avoiding drinking alcohol for 24 hours before and after your vaccination.”

Avoid stress

Stress has a profound effect on innmune work. Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol produccion and oxidative stress in the body.

Eat healthy food

Before you get your vaccine, you want to support your immune system. Focusing on healthy foods that are not heavily processed or refined may help your body stay healthy.

Get enough sleep

Getting sufficient, high-quality sleep on a regular basis strengthens your body's immune system and optimizes your response to a vaccine

Exercise or physical activity

Since regular exercise can help strengthen the immune system, it seems likely that it could also help protect from a severe or life-threatening case of COVID-19
